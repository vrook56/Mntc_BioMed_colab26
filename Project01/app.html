What is an infection?

An infection happens when a pathogen enters the body, survives, and begins to multiply. Common pathogens include bacteria, viruses, fungi, and parasites.

  • Pathogens enter through cuts, airways, food, water, or body fluids.
  • Symptoms can occur when the immune system responds.
  • Some infections are mild, while others need medical treatment.
